Rehabilitation Center

We designed a center of this type in the form of a useful idea for the community we are in, because Romania has deficiencies in terms of rehabilitation hospitals for drug users.

Important services

Unlike the already existing rehabilitation departments, the idea of ​​our center also involves offices specially dedicated to counseling and mental rehabilitation of those who have used drugs.

Implementation

Due to the fact that the members of the project are minors, the establishment of the center is almost impossible to achieve, so here is just a concept, a basic idea that could have results.
